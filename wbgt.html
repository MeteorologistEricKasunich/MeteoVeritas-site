Wet Bulb Globe Temperature (WBGT)

What is WBGT?
WBGT stands for Wet Bulb Globe Temperature — a heat stress metric combining temperature, humidity, wind, and solar radiation. It’s more accurate than the heat index, especially for outdoor work or exercise.

We apply the Liljegren method, the most accurate open-source WBGT approximation, which includes:
• Black globe temperature estimation
• Solar radiation & wind speed adjustments
• Sun angle and time-of-day effects

WBGT Risk Levels:
  • Low: WBGT below 82°F — Minimal risk
  • Moderate: 82°F to 87.9°F — Use caution, hydrate
  • High: 88°F+ — High risk of heat illness

WBGT data is estimated using Open-Meteo weather model inputs.
