Wet Bulb Globe Temperature (WBGT)

What is WBGT? WBGT stands for Wet Bulb Globe Temperature — a comprehensive index that estimates heat stress risk using temperature, humidity, wind speed, solar radiation, and time-of-day factors. We apply the Liljegren method, widely regarded as the most accurate open-source WBGT approximation.

  • Includes black globe temperature estimation
  • Adjusts for solar radiation, wind, and sun angle
  • Provides full-day hourly forecasted WBGT values

This data is forecasted hourly WBGT for the current day.

WBGT Risk Levels:
  • Low: WBGT below 82°F — Minimal risk
  • Moderate: WBGT 82°F to 87.9°F — Use caution, hydrate
  • High: WBGT 88°F+ — High risk of heat illness

WBGT data is estimated using Open-Meteo model inputs and the Liljegren method.
